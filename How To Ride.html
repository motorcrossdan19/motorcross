How To Ride A Dirtbike

To ride a dirtbike you must start the dirtbike.You can start your dirtbike by pulling out the lever on the bottom right side of the bike. Make sure the bike is in neutral, by down shifting a couple of times then slowly shift up a little. After the bike is in neutral you can pull the lever out and kick it back with alot of force, this may take more than one try if you are not used to it then slowly twist the throttle. Only if your bike has a push start, put the bike in neutral and press the button for 3 seconds and then slowly twist the throttle. After you have done all of those steps, hold the clutch which is the lever on the left after you pull it in twist the throttle and make sure you are holding onto the clutch the whole time. While holding the clutch shift the gear which is located on the lower left side of the bike down 1 and it will put you into first gear! after you are in first gear while holding the clutch wist the throttle half way and let go of the clutch, by then you will be riding! if you want to go faster hold the clutch and quickly shift up, slowing down is the same way just hold the clutch and down shift, another way to slow down is by using the front brake which is located on the right side of the handlebar, only use it when you are going straight, if you want to slow down by a turn use your back brake located on the lower right side of the bike, push it down slowly the bike may slide but that is okay. just lean forward. After you follow these steps you can ride a dirtbike!
