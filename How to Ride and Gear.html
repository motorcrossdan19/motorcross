Motorcross gear

The motorcross gear is important so that riders and racers do not get sevierly injured.

Helmet

Helmets are important so you do not get a serious head injury if you fall.

goggles

Goggles protect dust and other objects from entering your eyes.

jersey

jerseys are flexible shirts that are vented to keep you cool.

gloves

gloves are important ecause they protect your hands from sharp objects.

elbow pads

Elbow pads are important so you do not get a serious arm injury if you fall.

chest protector

Chest protectors are important so you do not get a serious injury if you fall.

neck brace

Neck braces are important so you do not get a serious neck injury if you fall.

Knee pads

Knee pads are important so you do not get a serious knee injury if you fall.

Pants

pants are important so you do not get a serious burn on your legs from the exaust.

Boots

boots are made so you dont burn your foot on the hot exaust
